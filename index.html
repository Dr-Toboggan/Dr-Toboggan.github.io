Introduction

Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio. I have seven years of professional experience in the Information Technology industry, as well as a Bachelor's Degree in IT. For the last three and a half years I've been a Network Engineer for a rural ISP. I was recently asked to resign due to my former employer closing its doors on my department. While I was initially disappointed by this outcome, it has prompted me to reevaluate these last seven years to determine what really matters to me regarding my career.

This time of reflection has made me realize that the most satifying points of my career haven't come from working with firewalls, configuring/maintaining servers, or troubleshooting intricate customer networks. Rather, my most fulfilling moments in IT came when I was creating software to help people, automating repetitive processes with scripts, and increasing organization productivity with my own code. I am very excited to be shifting gears to pursue my true passion: Software Development.
